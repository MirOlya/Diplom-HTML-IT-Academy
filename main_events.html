Html, css and js exam
IT-Academy in Minsk Educational Center for Programming and High Technologies

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript technologies are used to create the "front" side of a frontend web page. A web developer (layout designer) creates a website framework using HTML, and "decorates" it by adding different fonts, colors, and backgrounds, using CSS. But JavaScript helps to "enliven" the site, making it interactive.

At the basic course in HTML, CSS and JavaScript in Minsk, you will learn how to make web pages with "block" and "table" layout, create adaptive and mobile sites.

SVOBODY.4 entered the top 10% of the best restaurants in the world

Yesterday on Instagram of the SVOBODY wine bar.4 it became known that the institution received the Traveller's Choice award from Tripadvisor. If you traveled a lot before the pandemic, you're probably familiar with this app. Every year, the company awards the best restaurants, analyzing reviews and ratings of travelers, as well as adding restaurants to the "Favorites". So, the Minsk wine bar entered the top 10% of the best restaurants in the world.

No "chemicals" and semi-finished products. Healthy food bistro o'da opened in Minsk

Today, o'da bistro, specializing in proper nutrition, opened in Minsk. The creators claim that their restaurant is not about boring salads and the lack of bright flavors, but about balance and natural ingredients. We went there and told you what awaits you.

The restaurant is located in the residential complex "Mayak Minsk" at the address Mstislavets Street, 18 and is a logical continuation of the store that sells products for beauty and health. In fact, they occupy the same space and smoothly flow into each other.

The new Burger King on Zybitskaya distributes free burgers all day. Have Minsk residents gone crazy this time?

In the morning, another Burger King opened at Zybitskaya 4. Today, a free Whopper Junior burger will be added to any order here. However, only at the beginning of each hour. So far, there is no large multi-kilometer queue: everything is orderly, noble, without a crush. But who knows what will happen after lunch, when the students finally finish their lessons, and the students have a couple... Although let's be honest: we are unlikely to repeat the same hysteria when people came to the first restaurant of the chain for free drinks with five-liter bottles.

But not only calories will be bestowed all day in the restaurant to their customers. There are also paper sombreros and cactus magnets-you can get them by ordering something from the new Mexican burger line. So far, they are only available on Zybitskaya.
